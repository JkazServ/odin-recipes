Creamy Parmesan Pasta

Description

This is a simple yet tasty pasta dish that the whole family will enjoy. Add protein of your choice or swap out the pes for another veggie, this is truly customizable.

Ingredients

  • Pasta, pick your favorite shape
  • Parmesan Cheese, grated
  • Butter
  • Frozen Peas
  • Heavy Cream
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Cook pasta as directed by package.
  2. Pour frozen peas into colander and place in sink.
  4. Strain pasta directly onto peas then return to pot.
  5. Add butter, cheese, and cream to pot and stir until combined.
  6. Serve and enjoy
