Grilled Chicken Sandwich

Description

This is the best chicken sandwich you will ever eat. We use chicken thighs and coat them in a marinade made of mayo, buffalo sauce, and spices, before grilling the chicken to perfection. Then we build our sandwich with cheese, lettuce, tomato, and bacon.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Thighs (Boneless, Skinless)
  • Mayo
  • Frank's Red Hot
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • Buns
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Cheese
  • Bacon

Steps

  1. Preheat grill and oven.
  2. Once oven is preheated, add bacon on a baking sheet. Cook until crisp.
  3. Mix mayo, buffalo sauce, salt, pepper, and garlic powder together in a bowl.
  4. Add chicken to the bowl and mix to coat.
  5. Grill chicken for 3 minutes per side. Check for an internal temperature of 165 degrees before removing.
  6. While chicken is cooking, slice tomato and lettuce, and toast buns if desired.
  7. Layer ingredients on bun and enjoy.
